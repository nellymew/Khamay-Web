About Khamay

Your one-stop shop made with love by kamay!

Hi, Everyone! This is Khamay, your friendly neighborhood store located in Payatas, Quezon City, offering a perfect combination of professional printing services and a wide variety of anik-anik products.

Our printing services use modern equipment to ensure high-quality output for your documents, photos, and custom designs. Meanwhile, our anik-anik section is carefully curated by our kamay to provide unique finds at affordable prices.

We take pride in our fast service, competitive pricing, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you need to print important documents or want to have some cute anik-anik, Khamay is here to serve you with a smile.
